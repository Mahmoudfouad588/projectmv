About Us

Mountain View Call Center has been established in 2007 with a small group of employees, Covering all operational requests as well as supporting other departments through out the whole day by spilitting our team into two different shifts (Morning Shifts & Night Shifts) We started develop our team members to be more sales oriented and to grow bigger, Focusing on the clients needs and trying our absolute best to match the clients with their needs, Till we proudly reached a point were we are categorized as number one real estatte call center all over Egypt. "Those who cannot change their minds cannot change anything", We belive in Flexability, Commitment and self development, Because that is how we are going to continue succeding.
